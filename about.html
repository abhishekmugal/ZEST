About ZEST



ZEST – COEP’s Annual National Level Inter-Engineering Sports Meet gives the students a base to showcase their dexterity, talent and endurance. ZEST has emerged as one of the biggest and most awaited sports events for students. Its popularity is reflected from the immense support it has got over past 12 years with ZEST’14 attracting more than 10,000 athletes with over 150 colleges participating from around 11 states across the country including Maharashtra.

In its 13 edition, ZEST has widened its horizons with the addition of 2 new special events. ZEST’15 will host 30 different sports events with both men and women participation. ZEST will continue to attract growing participation from all over the country making it nation’s one of the most successful Sports Fests.
