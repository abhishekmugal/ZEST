Ball Badminton

Registration Fee: Boys - 400/- Girls - 400/-

Rules :
Team Size:

For Men & Women:
1. Playing: 5 (five) 2. Extras: 5 (five)
3. Total (Playing + Extra): 10 (ten)

1. The match shall consist of three games, each of 29 points. The team that takes the best of three games shall be declared as winners.
2. The choice of sides and the right of first service shall be decided by toss at the commencement of a match.
If the team winning the toss chooses the right of service, the other team shall have the choice of side and vice-versa.
3. The umpire shall be the sole judge of fair or unfair play and of the fitness of the weather and the light for play.
His decision shall be final.
4. The ball may be replaced by an umpire, if it is either lost or deformed.
5. All the matches will be the knockout matches.
6. In view of the umpire, if a player misbehaves in the court that brings discredit to the referee/other players/officials
lowering the image of the game, umpire shall warn the player. After the first warning the umpire will show yellow card at second instance.
This indicates that the player will be sent out of that match by showing a red card, at the third
instance when the team has to finish that game with the remaining four players only.
7. Every team should bring their own rackets.
8. All players should have Id-cards with them on the day of match.
9. If one team is not present on time at the venue of match, then the decision will be in favor of other team.

Last Rule: Referees decision will be final and binding.

Box Cricket

Registration Fee: Boys - 300/-

Rules
The tournament will be held at CoEP Ground.

1. The teams are expected to bring their proper Cricketing attire including their kits.
2.The tournaments committee reserves the right to change the venue, time and date of the matches if necessary.
The team should report 30 minutes before the scheduled matches.
3.If a team reports late, the team will be penalized as per the discretion of the umpire.
4. The rules as adopted shall apply, unless otherwise modified fully/partially and specified.
5. Each team will be given 20 minutes to bowl 05 over's and if exceeded will be penalized by the Umpires.
6. For every overstepped No-Ball or wide balls will be given 01 run extra.
7.There will powerplay of 01 over in which fielding restrictions will apply which will be explained just before the match.
8. If three continuous No-Balls, Wide balls and Dead balls are bowled then 4 extra runs will be given to batting team.
9. Only one bowler is allowed to bowl maximum 02 overs Decision of the Umpire will be final.
10. The squad should consist of 06 players.
11. The balls will be thrown overhand to the batsmen from half Pitch. The tournament will be played with soft Tennis ball.
12. All the hits outside the Box will be declared out. The tournament will be played on knockout basis.

All other details of the activity will be explained on the spot by the activity supervisors.
