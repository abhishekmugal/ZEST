Pre-Zest Event : UTKARSHA

A special Training programme of Football for the children from Patil Estate under 'Pankhudi'.
ZEST Team tried it's best to give an opportunity to these children to develop sportsman spirit by means of Football Training programme.
It was carried out successfully from 7th January to 13th January.

The children were provided with well equipped Football kit with enhanced training from our Football Coach.
