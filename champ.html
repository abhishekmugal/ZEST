CHAMPIONSHIP

“Champions aren’t made in the Gyms. Champions are made from something they have deep inside them – A Desire, A Dream, A Vision.”

Every year a ‘Zest Sports Championship’ title and trophy is awarded to ‘Best Sporting Contingent’s’ among participating institutes. Zest championship is meticulously refined point’s structure where each team receives point at quarterfinal stage and onwards. It has been seen over the years, that victorious team of maximum sports in Zest had a better chance of winning it and have won it as well. If any team shows any unethical behaviour on ground, their points will be deducted at the discretion of our faculty. Thus championship is not for winner but for true champions.
